Understoria is a mutual aid timebank for organizing communities. Post what you need, offer what you can, and track help in time credits — one hour of help equals one hour of help, whatever the task. No market pricing, no hierarchy, no algorithm deciding who gets support.

See the live demo Start a community

The demo is the real app, pre-filled with a sample community — it runs entirely in your browser, and only you can see it.

No landlords, no algorithms, no ads — self-hosted and owned by the people who use it.

Owned by the community

Each community runs its own node. There is no company in the middle.

Private by design

Your data is signed, encrypted at rest, and never sold, ranked, or mined.

Works offline

A local-first app on every device; syncing resumes on its own when a server is back.

What it does

The everyday tools of organizing, in one calm place.

Post what you need. Offer what you can. Build collective power — and see it add up.

🤝

Community board

Needs and offers across transport, food, childcare, skilled labour, emotional support, education, and more. Descriptions render a safe Markdown subset — no ads, no ranking, no feed.

🌱

Community projects

Collective efforts with task lists, starter templates, co-organizers, shifts, and a discussion thread per task. The In my care tab gathers everything you're carrying.

⏳

Timebank credits

Every exchange earns and spends time credits. New members start with seed credits, so asking for help is never gated.

📅

Shared calendar

Deadlines, expiries, and federated events — skillshares, potlucks, work days — in one agenda, with per-event .ics export. No tracking URLs, ever.

📊

A dashboard where the unit of progress is us.

Total hours exchanged, active members, solidarity streaks, and milestones — the community's story, not a personal scoreboard. No leaderboards; no one is ranked against anyone else.

Why it exists

Solidarity, not a marketplace.

Most "community" apps are surveillance with a friendly logo. Understoria is built on the opposite commitments — and they're enforced in the code, not just the copy.

One hour = one hour

Care, repairs, tutoring, a ride — all valued the same. No market pricing decides whose time is worth more.

No surveillance

No feed, no engagement metrics, no attendance graph, no selling of anything. Pull-only: nothing pushes at you.

Federated & resilient

Communities can mirror each other and grow more roots, so losing one server loses nothing.

Free software

AGPL-3.0. The complete source ships with every deployment — no lock-in, forkable forever.

How it works

A node, and an app that installs itself.

  1. 1

    Someone hosts a node

    A small server — an old laptop in a closet is enough. It holds the community's signed records and asks nothing of the outside world.

  2. 2

    Members install the app

    The app is a Progressive Web App — it installs from the browser, works offline, and keeps each member's own encrypted copy on their device.

  3. 3

    The community grows

    Invite by link, vouch for newcomers, and — when you're ready — grow another root or federate with neighbouring communities.
